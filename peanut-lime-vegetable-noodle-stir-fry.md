Peanut Lime Vegetable Noodle Stir-Fry
=========

Ingredients
---
 * 6 oz soba noodles
 * 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
 * 2 medium shallot
 * 2 garlic clove
 * 2 medium carrot
 * 2 medium bell pepper
 * 1/4 head of red cabbage
 * 1/4 cup unsalted roasted cashews
 * 3 Tbsp natural peanut butter
 * 1 1/2 Tbsp reduced-sodium soy sauce
 * 1 1/2 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
 * 1 Tbsp hoisin sauce
 * 1 Tbsp sriracha
 * 1/2 Tbsp agave syrup
 * 1/2 lime

garnishes
---
 * fresh cilantro
 * toasted sesame seeds


Directions
---
 0. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions.
 0. Add the oil to a large pan over medium-high heat. When hot, add the shallots and garlic. Cook for about 2 minutes, or until lightly golden.
 0. Then add the carrots, bell peppers, and cabbage. Cook for 2 more minutes.
 0. Meanwhile, add all the sauce ingredients to a mason jar. Mix.
 0. When the noodles are cooked, drain and add to the pan of vegetables. Pour the sauce mixture on top and toss to combine.
 0. Serve with the roasted cashews and garnish as desired. Enjoy!
